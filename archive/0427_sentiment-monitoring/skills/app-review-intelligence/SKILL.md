# App Review Intelligence

## Purpose

Convert raw app reviews into prioritized product insights.

## Core capabilities

- ingest and clean app review text
- classify sentiment and topic clusters
- detect recurring pain points and regressions
- map issues to feature requests/bug backlog
- track trend movement week-over-week

## Workflow

1. Collect review data by period/version.
2. Cluster by issue themes.
3. Score by frequency, severity, and recency.
4. Map to product backlog candidates.
5. Output roadmap-ready summary.

## Output format

1. Top pain-point clusters
2. Positive signals and retention drivers
3. Priority backlog recommendations
4. Release-quality risk signals
